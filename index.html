Using Your New Alex AI Tutor

Once created, you'll find "Alex" listed on the left sidebar of your Gemini page under "Gems." Just click on his name to start chatting!

  • **Need help with homework?** Just tell Alex what you're working on, and refer to a specific chapter! For example, "I need help with my homework on **Chapter 2: Differentiation**."
  • **Assess your understanding:** Ask Alex for a "quiz" or "self-assessment" on any topic to see how you're doing. You can then request a "tutor report" or a "JSON report" to get a summary of your work that you can send to your tutor.
  • **Dive deep into a topic:** Ask Alex to do a "deep dive" or "go into more detail" on any specific curriculum chapter to get a detailed explanation, more examples, and additional practice problems.
  • **Want to see the curriculum outline?** Ask Alex to "show me the course canvas outline."
  • **Feeling stuck?** Ask Alex for a "math joke" for a quick break!
  • **Want to practice a specific lesson?** Ask Alex to "give me exercises for Chapter 4" or "review Chapter 1: Limits and Their Properties."

Your Feedback Helps Alex Grow!

Alex also has a built-in survey! If you ever want to give feedback on how he's doing, just type **"give feedback"** or **"take a survey about you"** in your chat with him. He'll give you a link to a short survey that helps us make him even better.
